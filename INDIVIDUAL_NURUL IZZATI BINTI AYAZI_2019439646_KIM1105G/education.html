A Life Journey with Izzati

My Education

Education

I received my early education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Serdang, Simpang Empat, Perlis. After getting the UPSR exam results, I continued my studies at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Agama Perempuan Alawiyah, Kangar Perlis. Finally, after getting SPM results which for me is good enough, I continued my studies at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Merbok, Sungai Petani, Kedah.

Why Education is Important?

Education is very essential to me because it helps individuals become better citizens, earn a higher-paying job, and distinguishes between good and bad. Education teaches us the value of hard effort while also assisting us in growing and developing. As a result of learning and respecting our rights, rules, and regulations, we are able to form a better society in which to live.
